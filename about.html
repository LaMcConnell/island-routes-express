Our Founding Story

Island Routes Express was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to connect communities across the island through safe, reliable, and comfortable transportation. Recognizing the need for a bus service that prioritizes punctuality, passenger comfort, and accessibility, our founders came together to create a company that blends modern technology, well-maintained vehicles, and a customer-first approach. From humble beginnings with just a few routes, Island Routes Express has grown into a trusted transportation provider, committed to making every journey seamless and enjoyable for every passenger.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, reliable, and timely transportation while enhancing mobility and accessibility for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the leading island transportation provider, recognized for excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Services

Inter-City Bus Routes

Reliable and timely bus service connecting major towns and cities across the island.

Daily Scheduled Services

Comfortable Buses

Well-maintained buses equipped with comfortable seating and safety features for a smooth ride.

Corporate & Group Travel

Customized solutions for companies, schools, or tour groups requiring reliable group transportation.
